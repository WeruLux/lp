Startup Presentation - May 2026

Billions Travel, Human Performance Doesn’t

The Problem & The Gap

Jet lag and travel fatigue lead to reduced cognitive performance, poor sleep quality, and lower productivity & well-being. Yet today’s solutions are generic, static, and not personalized.

The Scale: With 4.5+ billion air passengers per year, 100M+ premium & frequent travelers are most affected. Millions cross time zones daily, disrupting sleep, cognition, and recovery.

The Gap: A global travel system built for logistics, not for human biology.

The Opportunity & Our Approach

The Opportunity: A new layer for travel: Real-time, AI-powered human performance optimization.

Our Approach: Werulux is an AI-powered travel platform that reduces jet lag and travel fatigue through real-time, personalized guidance before, during, and after flights. Our AI system orchestrates optimal timing for sleep, meals, light exposure, and recovery based on individual patterns and travel context.

  • We enhance in-flight rest through targeted light therapy to regulate circadian rhythms and acoustic stimulation to accelerate sleep onset.
  • We integrate physiological signals (EEG) to continuously refine recommendations based on real-time data.

How WeruLux Works

  1. Pre-Flight Assessment: Users complete a short, adaptive check-in capturing itinerary, sleep patterns, risk factors, and travel goals. WeruLux predicts jet lag risk and builds a personalized profile.
  2. Personalized Travel Plan: AI generates a tailored plan based on the user’s profile and stated goals (e.g., optimal sleep timing, light exposure, and behavioral adjustments).
  3. In-Flight Adaptive Guidance: Real-time recommendations adjust dynamically during the flight using physiological signals. Guidance continuously aligns with the user’s goals.
  4. Post-Flight Recovery Optimization: Werulux continues support after arrival, tracking recovery and refining guidance to restore circadian alignment faster.

Value Creation

  • Reduced jet lag and improved in-flight sleep quality.
  • Seamless, personalized travel experience across the entire journey.
  • Faster post-flight recovery and improved cognitive performance.
  • Differentiated premium experience through hyper-personalization.
  • Increased customer satisfaction and loyalty among high-value travelers.
