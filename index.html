The Technology

Neuroadaptive.
Real-Time.
Biologically Personalized.

WeruLux combines behavioral data, real-time EEG physiological signals, and advanced ML models to deliver the world's first truly neuroadaptive travel wellness system.

EEG-Driven Sleep Stage Detection Wearable EEG sensors classify sleep stages in real-time enabling interventions precisely timed to circadian windows, not guesswork.
Probabilistic Jet Lag Modeling A proprietary probability model generates risk scores from chronotype, route, departure time, and behavioral history with individual calibration over time.
Adaptive Feedback Loop The system continuously adapts to your responses, making each subsequent journey more effective than the last.
Continuous Calibration Recovery is passively monitored through phone metadata inactivity patterns, charging cycles, and accelerometer data, requiring no effort from the traveler while enabling continuous, seamless calibration.
Performance Impact Designed to be meaningfully better at protecting traveler well-being than static, one-size-fits-all alternatives - continuously learning, continuously improving.
